Deon Moolman

Yuppiechef: Senior Software Developer

Deon Moolman is a senior software developer at Yuppiechef.co.za, the leading e-commerce store in South Africa. He is a technology enthusiast, loves programming concepts and languages and has spent 14 years in development on websites, mobile applications, payment switches, warehouse systems, desktop software and development tooling. Occasionally spotted with an Arduino in the one hand and soldering iron in the other.

Follow @CmdrDats

On a scale of one
Yuppiechef delights people daily by delivering stunning products, handwritten cards and quality service. This is an architectural look at where Yuppiechef has come from and how we're dealing with rapid growth while still keeping our top-notch service levels. We'll explore some of our experiences and highlight a couple of key pieces that are helping us to scale with very limited resources.
