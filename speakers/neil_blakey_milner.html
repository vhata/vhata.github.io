Neil Blakey-Milner

Facebook: Production Engineering Manager

Neil Blakey-Milner is a Production Engineer in Facebook's Core Systems team where he tries to make the infrastructure that powers Facebook's infrastructure more reliable and operationally efficient. Previously he was an early employee and lead engineer at South African startups KnowledgeTree and Yola.
