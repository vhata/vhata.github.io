Conference Schedule

The conference will repeat its tried and tested formula in 2014. It will run over two days, with a total of 12 x 45 minute talks plus regular breaks to keep the legs from falling asleep and awesome lunches to feed the mind and body. There will be three social events starting with the Wednesday evening with a sponsored drinks event in the city. Then similar events on the Thursday and Friday nights. These are a chance to socialise and network with the speakers, organisers, and fellow conference attendees.

The schedule is available as a Google Calendar in the following formats:
